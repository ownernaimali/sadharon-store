Welcome to Sadharon Store. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. This includes:

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, and phone number when you register.
  • Delivery Info: Shipping address and contact details for order fulfillment.
  • Payment Info: We use secure third-party gateways (like SSLCommerz or bKash). We do not store your full card/wallet details on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data allows us to:

  • Process and deliver your orders.
  • Send order updates via SMS or Email.
  • Improve our website features and user experience.
  • Prevent fraudulent transactions.

3. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We only share info with:

  • Logistics Partners: Delivery companies (e.g., Pathao, RedX) to deliver your goods.
  • Payment Processors: To verify and complete your transactions.

Questions about Privacy?

If you have any questions, contact us at privacy@sadharonstore.com
